## 📖 About Philia

**Philia (φιλία)** is a modern mobile application designed to foster genuine human connections and combat urban loneliness, encouraging new, in-person interactions. Named after the Greek word for the love between friends and the warmth of companionship, the app facilitates spontaneous, same-day dining experiences between strangers.

> **Our mission** is to reduce the friction of meeting new people, turning a solitary meal into an opportunity for community.

---

## 💡 Core Concept & Design

Unlike traditional social networking, Philia focuses entirely on immediate, real-world interaction rather than endless virtual messaging.

* ⏱️ **High-Velocity Gatherings:** Designed for "2-hour spontaneous plans," helping users quickly form small, manageable groups (2–6 people) to gather at restaurants within hours.
* 🤖 **AI Coordinator (No DMs):** To remove the anxiety and premature judgment of messaging strangers, users **do not DM each other**. An AI Chatbot acts as the middleman to confirm logistics, handle "mood checks," and provide meeting instructions.
* 🎨 **Warm & Minimalist Aesthetic:** Clean, "Notion-style" layouts with soft pastel color palettes, heavily rounded cards, and playful iconography to evoke comfort and speed.

---

## 📱 Core Application Flow (The 3 Main Screens)

The app is structured around three primary navigation tabs designed for high velocity, ensuring a user can join a plan in **under 30 seconds**.

### 1. 🏠 Home (The Launchpad)
The default landing page is minimalist but highly actionable, removing decision fatigue.
* **Live Status:** Welcomes the user with a time-stamped greeting and a live tracker of active plans nearby.
* **Primary Actions:** Quick-access buttons to **Start a plan** and **Join a plan**.
* **Happening Soon:** A feed of immediate, high-compatibility suggestions directly on the dashboard, highlighting the restaurant, time, match percentage, and exactly *why* it was suggested.

### 2. 🗺️ Join (Map & Discovery)
The heart of Philia's spontaneity. This tab uses a hybrid UI combining a dynamic map with an AI-powered list view.
* **Smart Filtering:** A top search bar allows users to type specific cravings, paired with quick-tap filter pills for budget and vibe.
* **Interactive Map:** The map displays custom, pill-shaped pins over local areas, showing the restaurant and how many spots are left.
* **AI-Ranked List:** A bottom drawer slides up displaying active plans. The AI ranks these based on food preference, budget, and vibe compatibility.

### 3. 👤 Me (Profile & Trust)
The profile page visually represents a user's identity and reputation within the community, focusing on safety and compatibility over posed aesthetics.
* **Avatar & Identity:** Users utilize stylized animal avatars instead of realistic photos to reduce shallow judgment. The header displays age and verification status.
* **Reliability Metric:** A prominent, heavily emphasized card visualizes the user's community standing with a percentage ring chart. It clearly tracks their attendance record versus "flakes" (no-shows or last-minute cancellations).
* **Personality Tags:** Clean, easily readable sections outline the user's Bio, Food preferences, and preferred Vibe.

---

## 🛡️ Trust & Safety

Trust is directly integrated into the app's architecture and user incentives.

* 📊 **Reliability Scores:** User profiles openly display past meetings attended versus their "Flake Number" (last-minute cancellations or no-shows) to incentivize good community behavior.
* 🤝 **Small Group Caps:** Limiting gatherings to **6 people** keeps the dynamic manageable and welcoming for introverts.
* 💬 **Chatbot Intermediary:** Preventing direct user-to-user digital messaging before the meal drastically reduces the potential for harassment or awkward interactions.
